How to draw

  • Pinch your thumb + index fingers to start drawing.
  • Release the pinch to stop.
  • Press C or click Clear to erase.

Face Recognition

  • Upload a reference face via Enroll face.
  • When your face matches, status turns Authenticated.
  • Drawing is blocked until authenticated (unless you bypass).
